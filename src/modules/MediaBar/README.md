# MediaBar

MediaBar is a simple music control space inside WinTools.

It gives you one place to see what is playing, control playback, and follow along with lyrics without jumping between apps.

## What it does

- Shows the current song, artist, and album art.
- Lets you play, pause, skip, and go back.
- Can connect to your music sources and use what is currently active.
- Displays synced lyrics when they are available.
- Includes a compact mini-player mode for quick control.

## How it feels to use

You open MediaBar, pick your source (or let it auto-detect), and control music from one clean screen.

If lyrics are available, they update as the song moves so you can follow the current line.

## Everyday flow

1. Open MediaBar from the WinTools launcher.
2. Start music from your usual app or service.
3. Use MediaBar to control playback and view lyrics.
4. Switch to mini-player when you want a smaller control view.

## Quick note

Some features depend on the music service and the song itself (for example, not every track has lyrics).
